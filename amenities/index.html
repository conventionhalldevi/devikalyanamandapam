What's Included

World-Class Facilities

From the moment your guests arrive to the final farewell, every detail is taken care of.

Full Air Conditioning

The entire main hall is centrally air-conditioned to maintain a comfortable temperature regardless of the season. Kerala's tropical heat won't affect your guests' comfort even during peak summer months.

200+ Seating Capacity

The main hall comfortably accommodates over 200 guests with neatly arranged seating, clear sightlines to the stage or mandap, and wide aisles for easy movement during the ceremony.

Dedicated Dining Hall

A separate, well-ventilated dining hall seating 90 guests at a time ensures that sadya or meals are served in a smooth, organised rotation. This keeps the main ceremony uninterrupted.

Ample Parking

Spacious parking in the surrounding areas of Pallipparambukavu Road accommodates cars and vehicles for all guests. Hassle-free arrival and departure — no stress over parking logistics.

Professional Sound System

A quality PA system with wall-mounted speakers provides clear, balanced audio throughout the hall. Ideal for ceremonies, speeches, chendamelam, and background music during receptions.

LED Lighting

Elegant recessed LED downlights throughout the hall create a warm, vibrant ambience that's perfect for photography. The bright, even lighting makes every moment capture-worthy.

Raised Stage / Mandap Area

A dedicated raised platform for the mandap or stage setup ensures all guests have a clear, unobstructed view of the ceremony. Decorators can customise the backdrop to your preference.

Fire Safety Equipment

The hall is equipped with fire extinguishers and adheres to safety standards. Your guests' safety is always our priority during every event we host.

Separate Restrooms

Clean, well-maintained restroom facilities separate for men and women are available to ensure comfort and hygiene for all guests throughout the event duration.

FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before booking your event with us.

How do I book the hall?

You can call us on +91 97467 57245 / +91 94472 16060 / +91 98473 37007 or fill in the enquiry form on our website. We'll confirm your date and provide booking details.

Is outside catering allowed?

Yes, you may bring your own catering team. The hall does not provide catering services itself. Please note that only vegetarian food is permitted on the premises — no non-vegetarian items are allowed.

How far in advance should I book?

We recommend booking at least 3–6 months in advance for weddings and major events, especially during the Kerala wedding season (Nov–Feb). Contact us early to secure your preferred date.

Can I decorate the hall myself?

Yes, you are free to bring your own decorator. We allow access to the hall the evening prior to the event for setup. Please discuss this with our team when booking so we can coordinate timing.

Is the pricing all-inclusive?

The quoted rental price covers hall access (main + dining), sound system, LED lighting, and parking. Catering, decoration, and any external equipment are arranged separately by the booking family.

Is there overnight accommodation nearby?

Tripunithura and the nearby Ernakulam area have several hotels and lodges to suit all budgets. The hall is also well-connected to Kochi city, making it convenient for outstation guests.

What types of events are hosted here?

We host weddings (Thirukalyanam), engagements (Nichayathartham), wedding receptions, Seemantham, Annaprasanam, Upanayanam, birthday celebrations, and other auspicious Hindu family functions.
